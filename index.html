About Me

Hi, I'm Mukul Sharma — a storyteller who speaks through light, shadows, and fleeting moments. I don’t just take photos; I preserve emotions, turning ordinary instants into timeless memories.

It all began when I picked up my first camera at 16 to capture the vibrant streets of Delhi. What started as a hobby soon became a passionate profession. Today, I’ve had the privilege of photographing 200+ weddings, 50+ destination shoots, and collaborating with brands like Vogue India and Taj Hotels. I believe in authenticity over perfection. Whether it’s the unscripted laughter at a wedding or the raw beauty of a Himalayan sunrise, I aim to capture life as it unfolds — candid, real, and breathtaking.

— Mukul Sharma
